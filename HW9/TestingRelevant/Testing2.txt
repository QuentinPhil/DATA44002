Graduate school is a significant financial commitment, but the long-term benefits of an MS in Business Analytics (MSBA) from Wake Forest is worth the investment. Our close ties with our corporate partners ensures we train students on the most up-to-date analytics methodologies and market-relevant skills for career success and competitive compensation.

MSBA applicants are automatically considered for merit and need-based scholarship awards. In fact, most domestic students received some level of scholarship support last year.We are committed to providing financial assistance through both scholarships and various loan programs.
